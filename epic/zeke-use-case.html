Use Case

Zeke wants to be able to visit a website that will provide detailed information on recreational sites in New Mexico.
Because he's an avid outdoor enthusiast, he wants to be able to look up not only hiking trails, but also camping sites, mountain biking trails, fishing lakes, etc.
He would also like to be able to see reviews of the sites and find out which are the most popular and be able to leave ratings/reviews himself.

User Story

Zeke wants to be able to visit the best-recommended trails in the area for outdoor recreational activities and leave feedback on visited sites.

Interaction Flow

  • Zeke visits Google.com
  • Zeke searches "nm outdoors"
  • Search returns "NM-Outdoors.com"
  • Zeke clicks NM-Outdoors.com URL
  • He is directed to the site and is shown a navigation, search bar CTA, and general info/brand (featured sites)
  • Zeke clicks "log In"
  • He is directed to account log in page
  • He logs in and is redirected back to landing page
  • He clicks on "Hiking Trails Near Me"
  • Zeke is then directed to a page with a map with pins to sites that fit category and location
  • He clicks on a pin
  • A new tab opens with data/information on specific site
  • Zeke scrolls to see site description, map location, pictures, ratings, reviews
  • He clicks on "Rate/Review"
  • Text box opens with blank stars
  • He applies 5 stars to rate the site and leaves a text review
  • He then clicks "Submit" to post review
  • He clicks "Log Out"
  • Zeke is logged out and redirected to landing page
